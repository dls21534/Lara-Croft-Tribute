Lara Croft

The Adventurous, Puzzle Solving, Artifact Seeking Archeologist.

Here's a one of the few time lines of Lara Crofts's life:

  • 1968 - Born in Wimbledon, London.
  • 1971-1979 - Had a private tutor.
  • 1979-1984 - Attended Wimbledon High School for Girls.
  • 1984-1986 - Attended Gordonstoun Boarding School.
  • 1986-1989 - Attended Finishing School.
  • 1991-1996 - Found the Ark of the Covenant and many other artifacts and saw sightings of Big Foot.
  • 1996 - Discovered the Lost City of Vilcabama.
  • 1997 - Travels to China to search and find the Dagger of Xian.
  • 1998 - Traveled to India in search of the Infada Stone.
